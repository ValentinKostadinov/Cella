Introduction
Cella is an engine for running cellular automata (CA). The most well known CA is Conway's Game of Life.
Grid Navigation
Pan the grid by dragging the mouse or use the keyboard arrows.
Cell Editing
Right-click to toggle a cell. Right-click and drag to toggle multiple cells.
